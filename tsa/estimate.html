Tax Rebate - Near Exact Costs

Figure out more exact numbers on how much installing solar panels of your own will cost, you can use the Solar Estimator to learn more.

To use you need your zip code, address, and information about your house, such as the type of roof.

This can be a good way to see how much installation will cost before you go all in on solar panels.

It will also tell you how much money you would save if you were to go solar, and who doesn't like saving money?

Lastly, it will tell you how many solar panels you would need to cover your roof.
